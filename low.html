What is Starbucks® Rewards?

Starbucks® Rewards is our loyalty program that rewards customers in meaningful and exciting ways.

How do I join Starbucks® Rewards?

You can join Starbucks® Rewards by simply activating a Starbucks Card then registering to create a Starbucks Account. There is no registration fee, however, the minimum amount to activate a Starbucks Card is P300.

Members previously registered to My Starbucks Rewards are automatically transitioned to the new program. Members simply need to update their password on the website or app, download the latest version of the app and add a new Starbucks Card to their existing account.

How do I earn a Star?

You must pay with your registered Starbucks card (or your registered Card in the mobile app) in order to earn Stars. You will earn 1 Star for every Php25 spent on beverage, food or merchandise purchased from our stores. Stars cannot be earned on purchases of gift certificate, Starbucks Cards or Starbucks Card reloads.

Purchases in the official Starbucks online store on Lazada:
Once your Starbucks Rewards account has been successfully linked to your Lazada account, you will earn Stars based on the value of your purchase in the official Starbucks online store on Lazada using any type of payment – cash, debit card, credit card, Lazada wallet at the rate of 1 Star for every ₱40 spent.

What rewards can I redeem with Stars?

Every 100 Stars you collect converts to a Reward Voucher that can be used to redeem your choice of a handcrafted beverage of any size (excluding premium Starbucks Reserve coffee and ice cream beverages) or a food item from our bakery and cake menu. Add-ons in beverages will be paid for by the member. Only 1 reward can be redeemed per transaction.

How will I know when I am eligible to redeem a free reward?

Every 100 Stars earned is automatically converted to one (1) Reward Voucher. Reward Vouchers are automatically and electronically loaded to the member’s Starbucks Rewards account and has a validity of 90 days upon issuance (exact date and time). Members can view and track their available rewards and its expiry date and time on the Starbucks website or using the Starbucks PH mobile app.

Do Stars expire?

Yes. All Stars earned are valid for redemption for one (1) year, starting on the date of your registration. Stars that are not converted to Reward Vouchers will expire on your Anniversary Date (date of registration) each year. For members previously registered to My Starbucks Rewards, Anniversary Date is defaulted to July 16.

Do Reward Vouchers expire?

Reward Vouchers are automatically and electronically loaded to the member’s Starbucks Rewards account and is valid for 90 days from the exact date and time of issuance.

How do I check my Stars and Reward Voucher?

You may check your Stars and Reward Voucher earned via your account online or Starbucks PH mobile app. Star Tracker in the website and mobile app account will only show total full Stars accumulated by the member.

All partial Stars earned (rounded to two (2) decimal points, unless ending in zero which will be shown in 1 decimal point) for each transaction may be viewed by members in the Transaction History of their website or mobile accounts.

You can inquire about your Stars from any of our participating Starbucks stores, by calling (02) 8462-6262 (Metro Manila) or 1800-10-4626262 (provincial).

What are other member exclusive benefits?

Upon registration, you will automatically enjoy:
• Earn double Stars on your purchases made during the first seven (7) days of membership
• Earn double Stars on new beverages on its launch day
• Get notified and earn double Stars on your purchases on a random day every month
• A yearly complimentary handcrafted beverage or food item on your birthday*

*Handcrafted beverage of any size excluding Starbucks Reserve ice cream beverages.
* Food item includes bakery and cakes but excludes sandwiches, salads, pasta or bistro box. Member must join at least 7 days before birthday and make at least 1 qualifying purchase within the last 30 days. One-time use only.

How will I know if I am eligible to redeem my benefits?

You will be notified through email or mobile app inbox of the benefits that you are eligible to redeem. 

Double Star Registration Treat 
You are eligible to earn Double Stars on all eligible purchases starting on the day your register day until 7 days thereafter. The number of Stars they earn will be multiplied by two (2). 

Birthday Reward 
To qualify for the Birthday Reward, you must join Starbucks Rewards at least seven (7) days prior to their birthday and has at least one (1) Star-earning transaction within the past 30 days prior to birthday.  

Double Stars on Promotional Beverages                                                                                                 
Members are eligible to earn Double Stars on promotional drinks on the first day it is launched. The number of Stars they earn will be multiplied by two (2). 

Double Star Days 
Members are eligible for one (1) Double Star Day every month. Double Star day is random. Members will be notified through email or mobile app inbox of Double Star Day each month, which may vary from month to month. 
 

Do my other benefits expire?

Yes. Upon successful registration, your Double Stars registration benefit is valid only on the day you register until seven (7) days thereafter. Double Stars on promotional beverages are valid only on the first day of launch, while Double Star Days are valid only on the day itself.

Your Birthday Reward is valid for redemption starting 6am on your exact birthday until 6am seven (7) days thereafter. To qualify for the Birthday Reward, member must join Starbucks Rewards at least seven (7) days prior to their birthday and has at least one (1) Star-earning transaction within the past 30 days prior to birthday.

Will I be able to use my Starbucks Card at all times?

Yes, the Starbucks Card is an acceptable form of payment at our stores. However, we may temporarily be unable to perform most Starbucks Card transactions due to unavoidable technical problem or unforeseen network downtime.

When the network is down, Starbucks Card customers can still earn Stars on eligible purchases even if they pay using other forms of payment (cash, debit/credit card, eWallet). The transaction will be similar to a regular purchase using cash, debit/credit card or eWallet but will require our barista to scan your Starbucks Card to capture your account's information. The balance on your Starbucks Card will not be deducted. A purchase receipt will be issued with details of your Card as proof of an offline Starbucks Card transaction.

What should I do if my Starbucks Card is lost or stolen?

If you have multiple cards under your account, you can still use the remaining cards for purchase. You also have the option to transfer the balance of the lost/stolen card to one of your other cards registered under the same account.

What is an offline transaction?

When connectivity is down in stores, we are temporarily unable to perform all Starbucks Card transactions. To serve you better at times of downtime, we will allow you to earn Stars on your purchase even if you pay using other forms of payment (cash, credit/debit card, eWallet).

We can capture your Starbucks Card information even when we are offline and credit Stars to your account for eligible purchases. The balance on your Starbucks Card will not be deducted.

How will I know if the store processed my transaction offline?

Our barista should first attempt to process your purchase using your Starbucks Card. If the system is down, you will be asked if you would like to proceed with your purchase using other forms of payment (cash, credit/debit card, eWallet). The transaction will be similar to a purchase using cash, credit/debit card or eWallet, but will require our barista to scan your Starbucks Card at the register to capture your account's information. You will be issued a receipt for your purchase with details of your Starbucks Card.

When will my Stars from an offline transaction be posted on my account?

Stars earned from offline transaction will be credited to your account within seven (7) working days from the date of your transaction, unless prevented by some unavoidable technical problem, in which case, such stars will be credited to your account as soon as the technical problem is resolved.

How will I know if my account has been credited the Stars from an offline transaction?

All transactions may be viewed in your account via website or mobile app. Once offline transaction is successfully processed, the transaction details will appear in the Transaction History. Alternatively, you may also visit our stores or call the hotline to check if your Stars have been credited to your account.

What if my Stars are not reflected on my account within the 7-day turnaround time?

Please be ready with the receipt of your offline transaction and we would be happy to help you with your concern. We will request for Stars to be credited as soon as we have validated your transaction.

Can I return items or merchandise from an offline transaction?

Only purchases of defective merchandise may be refunded or exchanged within fourteen (14) days of purchase. You may return or exchange the item in any of our participating stores provided original receipts are presented. You will be refunded in cash for return of defective merchandise.

Can I request for an offline transaction even when there is connectivity?

No. The stores are only allowed to process offline transactions only when they are experiencing intermittent connection or system downtime.

Can customers do an offline load, reload, or redemption?

No. When connectivity is down in stores, our system is unable to determine your current balances and Stars earned. Thus, we are limiting offline transactions to earning of Stars for Starbucks Card customers who choose to pay with other forms of tender.

What is Mobile Order & Pay?

No time? No line. The Starbucks® Philippines mobile app for iPhone® or Android™ now allows Starbucks Rewards members to place an order and pay ahead before they reach the store. Mobile Order & Pay means you can walk in or park in a designated parking slot and then receive your freshly made order. The mobile ordering experience is seamlessly integrated into Starbucks® Philippines mobile app and Starbucks® Rewards program.

Where is Mobile Order & Pay available?

Mobile Order & Pay is available to use in all stores in the Philippines.

How do I get Mobile Order & Pay on my phone?

Gaining access to Mobile Order & Pay is easy, with only a few simple steps needed:

1. Download the latest Starbucks® Philippines app for iPhone® and Android™. If you are an existing user of the app, check the App Store or Play Store to ensure the app has been updated to the latest version.

2. Ensure location services are turned on to allow the app to display the eligible stores.

3. Register to become a Starbucks® Rewards member if you aren’t already, or log into your account. Customers first need to register as a Starbucks® Rewards member to use of Mobile Order & Pay.

4. Click on the "Order" option from the menu on the bottom panel of the screen and you are ready to use Mobile Order & Pay.

How do I place an order?

To use Mobile Order & Pay, customers follow five easy steps:

1. Click on the "Order" option from the menu on the bottom panel of the screen.

2. Select the beverage and/or food items to order: Just as in our stores, beverages are customizable, including numerous options to modify size, number of espresso shots, dairy selections and more. You can order a maximum of 15 items per mobile order.

3. Select the store for pick up: Approximate wait times will be viewable on the customer’s phone prior to selecting store location. Menu items will also be updated based on availability after the store is selected. Directions will also be available, if needed.

4. Select preferred the pickup option available in the selected store.

5. Confirm by clicking "Order". At the time of order, payment is made from the customer’s default registered Starbucks Card in the app.

6. Proceed to the selected Starbucks® store to pick up your beverages and/or food: Orders are immediately sent to the selected store where baristas will begin preparing the items. When baristas have finished your order, you will receive a push notification provided by Starbucks® Philippines app.

For in-store pickup, once at the hand-off area, tell the barista your name and present your order details to enjoy your mobile order.

For curbside, check-in in the app once at the designated parking slot. This will inform the barista to bring deliver your mobile order.
